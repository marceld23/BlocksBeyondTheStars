# Code of Conduct

**Blocks Beyond the Stars** is a family project, and we would love for it to stay a
welcoming place for everyone. The whole rule, really, is this:

> **Be kind to one another.**

That means:

- **Be welcoming.** People of every age, background and skill level are invited to play,
  report bugs and contribute. Beginners especially — everyone starts somewhere.
- **Be respectful.** Disagree about ideas, never about people. No personal attacks,
  harassment, insults, or discrimination of any kind.
- **Be patient and helpful.** Assume good intent, explain things kindly, and remember a
  lot of us are doing this in our spare time for fun.

If someone is making the project a worse place — being rude, harassing others, or otherwise
breaking the spirit above — please let us know.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that breaks this, contact the maintainers privately
through GitHub (open a private security advisory, or reach out to
[@marceld23](https://github.com/marceld23) directly). Reports are handled discreetly.
Maintainers may remove comments, issues and pull requests, or block contributors, that
don't follow this code.

## In short

Treat others the way you'd want to be treated. That's it. Have fun out there. 🚀

---

*This is a deliberately short, plain-language code of conduct. In spirit it follows the
widely used [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org); we just wanted
something everyone can read in a minute.*
